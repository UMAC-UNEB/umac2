Awarding of Certificates

Policy on Awarding Certificates

UMAC awards certificates to successful candidates according to the prevailing policies. These certificates are the official recognition of a candidate's achievement in the national examinations conducted by the Associatio.

Levels of Certification

The association is responsible for awarding certificates at the following primary and secodary school levels:

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE)
  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE)

Historical Background of Certification

Initially, certification was conducted under the East African Examinations Council (EAEC), which issued the East African Certificate. Following the dissolution of the EAEC, Uganda started conducting its own examinations, and the certificates were renamed to current UCE and UACE.

Since 1983, the Primary Leaving Examination section was transferred the Ministry of Education to UMAC, centralizing the award of certificates for all three levels under one body

Important Notice on Lost Certificates

Please note that UMAC does not issue duplicate certificates, a letter of verification of results is issued to serve the concerned candidate upon request.
