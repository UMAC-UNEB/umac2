Welcome to UMAC

Uganda Marking Association Centre

Thank you for visiting the official website of the Uganda Marking Association Centre (UMAC). We are the national assessment body for national schools exams in Uganda, trusted by learners, educators, and institutions across the country.

Our work ensures that every Ugandan student receives fair, reliable, and globally recognised certification — opening doors to opportunity at home and abroad.
