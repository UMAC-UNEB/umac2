UMAC Normal Registration Period Extended to 30th June 2026

The management of the Uganda Marking Association Centre (UMAC) has officially extended the deadline for normal candidate registration across all assessment tiers. This extension is aimed at catering to new centers finalized in the system and ensuring all eligible candidates are properly enrolled ahead of the upcoming examination cycle.
