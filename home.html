Who We Are

A Recognised Centre of Globally Competitive Assessment

The Uganda Marking Association Centre (UMAC) is the assessment body responsible for conducting regional MOCK examinations in Uganda. Our strategic direction is built around four core pillars designed to serve every learner with integrity.

🎯 Our Vision

A Recognised Centre of Globally Competitive Educational Assessment and Certification.

🚀 Our Mission

To conduct valid, reliable and fair national examinations for the purposes of certification and selection.

🤝 Teamwork

Collaborating across directorates to deliver seamless, credible assessment services.

💡 Innovativeness

Embracing technology and research to continuously improve the quality of assessment in Uganda.

A Message from the Executive Director

Leading with Purpose and Integrity

Thank you for visiting the official website of the Uganda Marking Association Centre (UMAC). We are the regional assessment body for regional school examinations in Uganda. The key pillars of our strategic direction are built around professionalism, accountability, teamwork and innovativeness.

In a bid to serve the nation better, UMAC continues to invest in systems, people and processes that uphold the integrity of Uganda's examination results—ensuring that every certificate issued reflects genuine merit and effort.

AISU JOSHUA. Executive Director, Uganda Marking Association Centre
