Registration of Candidates

The e-registration process is designed to be simple and requires minimal computer skills. The system allows centers to register candidates from a location without internet connectivity and then send data to UMAC from a loaction where internet services are available.

Guidlines for Registration of Candidates

This process involes the use of electrical methods to carry out registration of candidates and these include the following:

  • Window-based desktop application.
  • On-line registration.
  • Ensure all candidate details are captured correctly and accurately before final submission through the web portal.

As the first step, the Association has designed and developed a window-based desktop system for electronically registering UCE and UACE candidates.

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS

For proper utilization of the e-Registration program, a School/Centre should have the following:

  • Digital photographs of Candidates
  • Computer with Windows Operating System
  • Internet Connection
  • A trained person to do e-Registration

SENDING DATA TO UMAC

All the required information for registration of candidates should be entered in the TEMPLATES provided.

Important Notice: UMAC Normal Registration for 2026 has been extended. Ensure all candidate data is uploaded by 20th August, 2026.
